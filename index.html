Prof. Marcos José Canêjo Estevão de Azevedo

received his Ph.D. in Computer Science from Universidade Federal de Pernambuco (UFPE) in 2022, his M.Sc. degree in Systems Engineering from University of Pernambuco in 2016, and his B.Sc. Degree in Computer Science from Catholic University of Pernambuco in 2013. He is a Professor at the Catholic University of Pernambuco, UNICAP, Brazil. His research interests include digital image processing and computational intelligence.
